    User Registration & Profile Creation

    Allow users to create accounts and set up profiles. Gather basic information like age, gender, weight, and fitness goals.

    Goal Setting

    Let users set fitness goals such as weight loss, muscle gain, or running distance. Provide motivational feedback and estimated time for results.

    Health Tips & Educational Content

    Share informative articles, videos, and tips on exercise, nutrition, and overall well-being. Provide guidance on exercises to avoid injuries and make healthy lifestyle choices such as meal plans.

    Interaction & Engagement

    Engage users in interactive conversations about their health and fitness journey. Respond to user queries, provide answers to common fitness-related questions, and offer guidance.
